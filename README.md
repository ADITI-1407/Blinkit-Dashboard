🚀 Power BI Project Showcase – Blinkit Dashboard
Excited to share my recent Power BI dashboard project based on Blinkit data, where I focused on transforming raw data into meaningful business insights 📊
🔹 Key Highlights:
• Created interactive visualizations to explore sales performance
• Designed and tracked important KPIs:
– Total Sales
– Average Sales
– Number of Items
– Average Rating
• Performed data cleaning & quality checks for accurate analysis
• Built DAX measures & calculations to derive insights
• Developed visuals like Donut Chart, Bar Chart, Stacked Column Chart & Line Chart
🔹 Business Insights Covered:
• Impact of fat content on total sales
• Performance analysis of different item types
• Comparison of sales across outlets
• Influence of outlet establishment on revenue
🔹 Project Workflow:
• Requirement Gathering
• Data Walkthrough & Connection
• Data Cleaning & Modeling
• DAX Calculations
• Dashboard Design & Development
• Insights Generation
